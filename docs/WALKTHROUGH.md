# The Pilgrim's Road — Complete Walkthrough

> ⚠️ **Total spoilers.** Every puzzle solution in the game. Ask Gus first — his
> third whisper in each room gives the answer anyway.

## Room I — The Oubliette

1. Take the **tallow candle stub** (glinting in the straw) and the **bent dose-spoon**
   (on the bread board). You cannot leave without them.
2. Examine the verse, the three chalk murals, the chains, and the **sun-mark beside
   the grate** (7 rays, letter **R** — auto-journaled).
3. The verse lists crows, rats, chains. Counts: **7 crows** (gallows mural),
   **4 rats** (loaf mural), **2 chains** (wall).
4. Set the grate rings to **VII / IV / II** → the grate opens. Descend.

## Room II — The Guard Room

1. Take the **iron crow** (by the brazier) and **flint & steel** (on the table).
2. Read all four clues: roster ("Wolf neither first nor last"), tally board ("first
   watch to the tusked"), table carving ("Serpent directly after Boar"), Osric's
   letter ("Falcon directly after Stag; not Dead of Night").
3. Examine the **sun-mark above the bolt rack** (3 rays, **A**).
4. Deduce: Boar=1, Serpent=2; Stag/Falcon adjacent; Wolf not last → Wolf=3, Stag=4,
   Falcon=5. Draw the bolts: **Boar → Serpent → Wolf → Stag → Falcon**.
5. The cabinet is empty but drafty: **hold the iron crow, click the cabinet back** —
   the false panel tears open. Descend.

## Room III — The Chapel

1. Examine the window (lilies: Cuthbert 1 + crook, Edmund 2, Agnes 3, Dunstan 4,
   Brendan 5) and the plaque ("sing as the lilies bloom; the shepherd sings first
   and last").
2. Examine the **sun-mark on the crypt arch** (4 rays, **U**).
3. Ring the bells: **Cuthbert, Edmund, Agnes, Dunstan, Brendan, Cuthbert**
   (**C-E-A-D-B-C**). The reliquary opens.
4. Take the **silver key** and **holy oil**, then exit by the cloister door.

## Room IV — The Scriptorium

1. It is too dark to read. Combine **flint & steel + candle stub** → lit candle;
   use it on the **desk sconce**.
2. The pen case holds the cipher strip: **2:4 · 5:1 · 1:6 · 7:3 · 3:2** — "line,
   then word; first letter" — and a comet-over-tower sketch.
3. On the shelf, only the **comet-and-tower tome** (the Chronicle) opens. Reading it
   journals **sun-mark 4** (the illuminated O of line 5 — 6 rays, **O**).
4. Index the page: 2:4 "no"→N, 5:1 "Old"→O, 1:6 "ravens"→R, 7:3 "tunnel"→T,
   3:2 "hall"→H. Chest dials: **N-O-R-T-H**.
5. Take the **still-room key**, **recipe right half**, and **Edmund's confession**.
   Exit by the stair door.

## Room V — The Still-Room

1. Take the **recipe left half** (behind the loose shelf board) and the **meat
   shank** (ceiling hook). Combine the halves → full recipe.
2. Examine the chart (Papaver=dreams, Valeriana=old boots, Mel=honey; widdershins =
   against the sun), the spoons (notches = measures; the 1-notch spoon is your bent
   spoon), and the **sun-mark branded on the mantel** (8 rays, **A**).
3. Brew at the kettle: **Papaver ×3** (3-notch spoon), **Valeriana ×1** (bent
   spoon), **Mel ×2** (2-notch spoon) → stir **against the sun, exactly 3 times**
   (the dial's engraved arrow points clockwise — go the other way) → light the
   hearth → pump the bellows until the **first whistle**, then **draw off**.
   Never touch the Cicuta (hemlock).
4. Combine **draught + meat shank** → drugged meat. Climb the stairs.

## Room VI — The Great Hall

1. **Hold the drugged meat, click the mastiff** — it sleeps.
2. Click the Wheel; the gears are rusted: **hold the holy oil, click the Wheel**.
3. Examine the fresco (tower/raven/key/comet) and the plaque ("no hand turns one
   wheel alone"), and the **sun-mark in the floor** (5 rays, **R**).
4. The Wheel: A turns rings 1+2, B turns 2+3, C turns 3+4. Rings need 2,3,3,2
   quarter-turns. Pull **A twice, B once, C twice** (any order). The tapestry door
   opens.
5. Take the **crank handle** from the alcove peg. Descend.

## Room VII — The Gatehouse

1. Ignore the great windlass — the main gate is watched (it is a signposted trap).
2. The plaque: "SIX SUNS LIGHT THE PILGRIM'S ROAD. THE FEWEST RAYS SPEAK FIRST."
3. Sort your journaled suns by ray count: 3-**A**, 4-**U**, 5-**R**, 6-**O**,
   7-**R**, 8-**A** → set the dials to **AURORA**. The north door opens.
4. In the tunnel: **crank handle into the square socket**, **iron crow through the
   drum spokes** (as the pawl), then **crank three times**. The water-gate lifts.
5. Dive. Dawn does not find you.
