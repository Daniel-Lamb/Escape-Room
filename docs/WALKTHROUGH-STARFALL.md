# Starfall Station — Complete Walkthrough

> ⚠️ **Total spoilers, including the ending.** Ask Gus first — his third transmission
> in each deck gives the answer anyway.

## Deck 1 — Cryo Bay

1. Take the **magnet stylus** (tool drawer). It is not junk. It is never junk.
2. Wipe the frost from gauges **A (4), B (7), D (2)**. Gauge C is cracked; the manifold
   plate says **A + B = C + D**, so C = 9.
3. Log **shard 1** from your own pod's data slot (6 peaks, U).
4. Thaw-lock code: **4-7-9-2**. Exit to Hydroponics.
5. (Optional but recommended: examine your pod, your sleeve, and the pod glass. Remember
   what they say.)

## Deck 2 — Hydroponics Ring

1. Open the **pipe access panel**. Tiles rotate **clockwise only**.
2. Route the flow: inlet → right along the middle row → up at column two → across the
   top row → down at the far corner → out to the return. The loop fills green.
3. Take the **UV grow-lamp** from the blooming rack; log **shard 2** on the nutrient
   tank (3 peaks, A). Exit through the iris door.

## Deck 3 — Crew Quarters · Med Bay

1. Read **Ibarra's datapad**: his one code is "the year we lost Aurora Station."
2. Find the **memorial plaque**: AURORA STATION — LOST **2085**.
3. Hold the **UV lamp**, click the med locker: prints glow on **2, 0, 8, 5**.
4. Enter **2085**. Take the **biogel** and **blue keycard**.
5. Log **shard 3** on the neural-lab tray (5 peaks, E). Read the manifest. Try the scale.
6. Badge the core access door with the keycard.

## Deck 4 — AI Core

1. Hold the **magnet stylus**, click the breaker panel — extract the fused relay slug.
2. The etched logic: **DOOR = A ∧ ¬B ∧ (C ∨ D)**, **ALARM = B ∨ (¬C ∧ D) ∨ (C ∧ D)**.
   D is poison (either alarm term catches it), so: **A=1, B=0, C=1, D=0**.
3. Read the cradle log. Log **shard 4** by the socket (2 peaks, W). Exit the bulkhead.

## Deck 5 — Observation Deck

1. The dome ghosts: `P: ▲•` · `Y: •◆◇` · `R: ○`. The calibration card:
   ▲=1 •=2 ◆=8 ◇=4 ○=0.
2. Attitude control: **PITCH 12 / YAW 284 / ROLL 0**. The deck rights itself.
3. Read the **comms console** — journal the RV-7 vector: **AZ 117 / EL 43**.
4. Log **shard 5** in the telescope housing (4 peaks, K). Take the companionway down.

## Deck 6 — Reactor Control

1. Hold the **magnet stylus**, click the bus panel — pull the slagged fuse.
2. Cells 40/25/15/10, max two per bus: **40+10 → LIFE SUPPORT (50)**,
   **25 → DOOR SERVOS**, **15 → UPLINK PRE-CHARGE**.
3. Click the panel again to take the ejected **charged capacitor**.
4. Log **shard 6** in the coolant manifold (7 peaks, P). Exit the servo door.

## Deck 7 — Pod Bay → Uplink Array (the twist)

1. Approach the pod. The biometric arch: **NO ORGANIC SIGNATURE DETECTED.**
2. Try again. The pod launches — empty, saving itself.
3. Click **Your hands**. Read everything. Choose to stand up.
4. Through the rear bulkhead (MAINTENANCE ONLY — it always would have opened for you):
   - **Biogel** on the cracked emitter.
   - **Charged capacitor** into the socket.
   - Passphrase — sort the shards by wave-peaks, fewest first:
     W(2) A(3) K(4) E(5) U(6) P(7) → **WAKEUP**.
   - Dish alignment: **AZ 117 / EL 43** (from the Deck 5 comms log).
5. Say yes to Gus. Press **TRANSMIT**.
