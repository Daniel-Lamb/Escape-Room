# The Gate of Life — Complete Walkthrough

> ⚠️ **Total spoilers, including what's in the alcove.** Ask Gus first — his third
> growl in each chamber gives the answer anyway.

## Chamber 1 — The Carcer

1. Take the **strigil** from the straw pallet. It is bathhouse junk. Keep it anyway —
   it is this game's bent spoon.
2. Read **Felix's verse** on the wall: *"Read me down the margin."* The line initials
   spell **A-R-E-N-A**.
3. Set the door's five letter tumblers to **ARENA**. The chain falls.
4. Hold the strigil, click the **drain grate** — lever it up and pocket **tessera 1**
   (egg-helm, S).
5. Exit through the door. (Gus will send you back for the drain if you skipped it.)

## Chamber 2 — The Armamentarium

1. Read the **dummy's tag** (SECVTOR — "the chaser"), the **mural**, and the
   **drill-master's tablet**. Examine the **shield rack** for the two-hands wear on
   the tall scutum.
2. Dress the dummy: **smooth rounded helm · tall scutum · straight gladius**.
   (Crestless per the mural; the brimmed visor is at the smith; sica is the griffin's,
   trident the net-man's, spear the hoplomachus's.)
3. The locker drops open: take the **dolabra** and the **wool rag**.
4. **Tessera 2** (spear, M) glints in the whetstone trough.

## Chamber 3 — The Shrine of Nemesis

1. Altar rite: exactly **IIII heminae**, one pour. Jugs are V and III.
2. Fill V → pour V into III (V keeps **2**) → empty III → pour the 2 into III →
   refill V → pour V into III until full (takes 1) → **V holds 4**. Pour into the lamp.
3. The niche opens: take the **oil flask**. Take the **bronze mirror** from the votive
   pile. The crooked votive tablet hides **tessera 3** (net, I).
4. Read the **doorpost charm** — the Sower's square, first line **SATOR**, "reads the
   same four ways." You will want this next door.
5. Optional now, required eventually: hold the **wool rag**, click the **oil flask**
   in your loculus → **oiled rag**. Gus suggests it for a reason.

## Chamber 4 — The Lanista's Tablinum

1. Hold the **strigil**, click the desk's re-waxed tablet: beneath the wax, **AREPO**
   — the square's second line, in Felix's hand.
2. The strongroom frame: row 3 is riveted **TENET**; "QVATTVOR VIIS LEGITVR" — it
   reads by four roads. Row 4 = row 2 backward, row 5 = row 1 backward:

   ```
   S A T O R
   A R E P O
   T E N E T
   O P E R A
   R O T A S
   ```
3. Inside the strongroom: the **winch key**, **tessera 4** (griffin, I), and the
   **lanista's ledger** — read it. Both lines. That is the whole story.

## Chamber 5 — The Hypogeum

1. Hold the **bronze mirror**, click the **niche** by the Lion Gate: Felix's chalk
   plan lights up and is journaled.
2. Enter the maze. Sinistra/Dextra only turn you; **only Perge moves**. You start
   facing north (the dark). Follow the plan exactly:
   **Perge ×2 · Dextra · Perge ×2 · Sinistra · Perge · Dextra · Perge** → the stair.
   (Wrong rooms are occupied; a den scramble sends you back to the gate but the map
   remembers.)
3. **Tessera 5** (palm frond, O) is under the trough in **Gus's old cage** — read the
   name board while you're in there.
4. Take the **crank handle** from the broken capstan. Up the stair.

## Chamber 6 — The Great Winch

1. **Winch key** on the brake. **Crank handle** in the capstan socket.
2. Felix's beam note: the lift asks **twelve men**; drum's mark × block's sheaves,
   exactly. Drums II·III·V, blocks I·II·IIII.
3. Mount the **III drum** and the **four-sheave block** (3 × 4 = 12). Heave.
4. **Tessera 6** (fish-crest, S) glints in the grease pit. Ride the platform up.

## Chamber 7 — The Porta Sanavivaria

1. Click the gate any time to hear what it still refuses.
2. **Oiled rag** on the seized hinge (combine wool rag + oil flask if you never did).
3. **Dolabra** on the bar seat — pry the great bar upright.
4. **Dolabra** on the half-bricked arch. Inside: Felix. Read his last tablet. Take
   the **rudis**. Gus will not leave without this, and neither should you.
5. The wicket's six rings, and above them the procession frieze: **spear-man ·
   net-man · egg-helm · fish-crest · griffin · palm-bearer.** *"As they march, they
   speak."* Match your tesserae's emblems to the marchers:
   spear **M** · net **I** · egg-helm **S** · fish **S** · griffin **I** · palm **O**
   → **MISSIO** — the word the crowd shouts when a life is to be spared.
6. Open the Gate of Life. Walk out with the lion. **MISSIO — GRANTED.**
