# The Wild Court — Complete Walkthrough

> ⚠️ **Total spoilers, including the verdict.** Ask Gus first — his third counsel in
> each trial gives the answer anyway (except the Tithe Hall, where the tithe moves and
> even Gus will only teach you the method).

## Trial 1 — The Sinkhole Nave

1. Take the **machete** (half-buried in the mud) and **your survey map** (fallen satchel).
2. Read the torn **field-guide page** — it is the key to the gate.
3. Examine your **compass** (spinning) and the **rim blazes** (grown over). Journaled;
   remember them.
4. The root-gate's three traces: pug mark with no claw tips = **JAGUAR**; three round
   toes = **TAPIR**; the smooth drag-line is no footprint at all = **BOA** (nothing
   *walked* there).
5. Set the sockets **JAGUAR · TAPIR · BOA**. Take **token 1** from the lintel
   (the boa, "N"). Exit east.

## Trial 2 — The Ferry Pool

1. Take the **braided vine cord** from the near post.
2. Click the **fledgling** once — listen carefully to whose voice it borrows.
3. Examine all three charges to learn their natures: the kit eyes the bird; the bird
   eyes the figs.
4. The crossing (coracle carries you + ONE charge):
   **bird over → back empty → kit over → bird BACK → figs over → back empty → bird over.**
5. On the far bank, take **token 2** from the post (the mantis, "P"). Up the stairs.

## Trial 3 — The Painted Grove

1. Read the three **carved laws** and both **murals**; examine the bark map itself
   (look closely at that river bend).
2. Take the **ochre pigment pot** and **token 3** (the tree-frog, "O") from the shelf.
3. Open the easel. The clans are painted correctly — the *borders* are wrong:
   - **Raze** the two cross-passes: **Heights–Scar** (tapir beside tapir) and
     **River–Fern** (macaw beside jaguar).
   - **Raise** all four ring borders: River–Heights, Heights–Fern, Fern–Scar, Scar–River.
   (Every ground ends with exactly three neighbors; the Crown binds to all.)
4. Press the **judge-stone**. Exit through the parted trunks.

## Trial 4 — The Totem of Teeth

1. Hold the **machete**, click the **strangler vines** on drum III.
2. Read the dais line: *the Court reads from the sky downward; each face takes the meal
   directly beneath it; no face speaks twice.*
3. The four reliefs are evidence of meals: shadow-cat fur in the sky-nest (**harpy ate
   ocelot**); coil bones in the shadow-cat's den — and *nothing smaller* (**ocelot ate
   boa**, the load-bearing clue); a singer's shape in the shed skin (**boa ate
   tree-frog**); a dancer's wing on the singer's tongue (**tree-frog ate mantis**).
4. Set the door column, top to bottom: **HARPY · OCELOT · BOA · TREE-FROG · MANTIS**.
   Wake the Totem.
5. The amber eye falls — the **amber lens**. Take **token 4** from the dais (the
   tamarin, "Y" — yes, that one is Gus). The order note is journaled automatically.

## Trial 5 — The Morpho Gallery

1. Take the **beeswax lump** (fallen comb). Examine the **procession border** — look at
   what hangs over the human figures. Take **token 5** from the niche (the harpy, "C").
2. Hold the **amber lens**, click the mosaic — the faded left wing blazes back.
3. Complete the right wing as the left wing's **reflection** (outer stays outer, top
   stays top): top-outer **azure teardrop**, top-inner **gold disc**, mid-outer **violet
   oval**, mid-inner **azure disc**, low-outer **gold teardrop**, low-inner **violet
   disc**. Wake the Wing.
4. (Optional, satisfying: combine **beeswax + ochre** now — Gus approves of notaries.)

## Trial 6 — The Tithe Hall

1. Hold the **vine cord**, click the Scale's broken pan.
2. Take **token 6** (the ocelot, "A") and **Vance's brass compass** (read the engraving:
   T. VANCE — 1911).
3. The Scale answers **twice** per rite, and the tithe is **re-laid every time the rite
   restarts** — so deduce, don't guess:
   - Weigh **three against three**. Balance → the lie is in the two held back; tilt →
     it's on the **lighter** side.
   - Then **one against one** of that group. Balance → it's the third.
4. **Present the lie.** The true pods sink; keep the **hollow pod**. Exit the unbarred
   door. (On a fresh run with no resets, the first rite's hollow pod is **VI**.)

## Trial 7 — The Verdict Roots (the twist)

1. Touch the **Speaking Stone**. Let it finish. Let *Gus* finish — advocate, accused,
   collar and all. He never was speaking in metaphor.
2. The rite, in order:
   - **THE LIE** — place the **hollow pod** on the Stone.
   - **THE WORD** — the tokens, ordered *as the totem eats* (harpy C, ocelot A, boa N,
     tree-frog O, mantis P) with *the advocate last* (tamarin Y): **CANOPY**.
   - **THE MAP** — your **survey map** on the Stone; then the **oath-seal paste**
     (beeswax + ochre, combined in the satchel). You redraw the valley against Vance's
     compass edge: swamp, scree, nothing worth felling. Seal it.
   - **THE WAY** — walk through the parted roots.
3. **THE COURT RULES IN YOUR FAVOR.**
